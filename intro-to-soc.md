# Intro to SOC - Writeup

## Objective
Understand the role of a SOC Analyst and security monitoring.

---

## Key Concepts

### SIEM
A platform used to collect, correlate, and analyze logs.

### Logs
Provide visibility into system and user activity.

### Incident Response
Process of detecting, analyzing, and responding to security incidents.

---

## Analysis Perspective
A SOC analyst monitors events, identifies suspicious behavior, and investigates patterns.

---

## What I Learned
- SOC work is based on monitoring and analysis
- Logs are essential for detection
- Detection requires thinking, not just tools
